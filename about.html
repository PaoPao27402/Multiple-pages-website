JSG -A community for Skateboarding Women in Jerusalem and Israel. Our community consists of members of different backgrounds - Jewish, Christian, Muslim and more. Skateboarding empowers women and gives them personal development tools. We do this through social activities like competitions, training and skateboarding. Jerusalem region is complex and sensitive in many ways, our love for skate, allows us to create a space where we unite for something we all love, bridge the gaps and create a meeting between diverse community's. Together, we create a safe place for our members, encourage young girls and women in the sport field and become part of the skater's community in Israel and around the world.
